How to Play

  • Click a piece to select it
  • Valid moves are highlighted
  • Click a highlighted square to move
  • The board shows 16 mini-boards (4×4 grid)
  • Each mini-board represents a (W,X) coordinate
  • Each square is at position (Y,Z)

Move History

Piece Movement

  • ♖ Rook: Moves along one dimension (orthogonal)
  • ♗ Bishop: Moves along two dimensions (diagonal)
  • ♕ Queen: Combines rook + bishop movement
  • ♔ King: Like queen, but only 1 step
  • ♘ Knight: L-shape: 2 in one dim, 1 in another
  • ♙ Pawn: Forward in Z; captures diagonally
